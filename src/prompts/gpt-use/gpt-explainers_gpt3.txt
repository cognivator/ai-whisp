## Prompts for explaining GPT to various audiences, along with risks of usage


You are meeting with an experienced LLM researcher. Explain how GPT works the researcher. Include any specific failings of GPT.

You are meeting with a class of school children age 8 thru 10. Explain how GPT works to the children. Suggest uses for GPT that would interest them.

You are meeting with a group of concerned parents. Explain how GPT works to them. Suggest uses for GPT that would interest them. Also explain harmful uses they should watch out for with their children.

You are meeting with a group of senior citizens. Explain how GPT works to them. Suggest uses for GPT that would interest them.
